Birds Gonna Be Happy

Written by Timbur Hood and illustrated by Mark Body, this book is a tribute to the beauty and resilience of these winged creatures.

Whether you're a seasoned birdwatcher or simply love nature, "Birds Gonna Be Happy" is sure to uplift your spirits and inspire a newfound appreciation for the magic of flight. So come soar with us – because with birds around, happiness is always in the air.

$45.00

"Birds Gonna Be Happy" is a charming ode to the avian world, filled with vibrant illustrations and heartwarming tales that capture the essence of our feathered friends. In this delightful book, readers are invited to embark on a journey through the enchanting realm of birds, where every flap of a wing and melodious chirp brings joy to the world. From the majestic eagle soaring high above the mountains to the tiny hummingbird darting among flowers, each bird has a story to tell and a lesson to impart.

Through lyrical prose and captivating artwork, author Timbur Hood and illustrator Mark Body celebrate the beauty, resilience, and sheer exuberance of these winged creatures. Whether you're an avid birdwatcher or simply appreciate the wonders of nature, "Birds Gonna Be Happy" is sure to lift your spirits and leave you with a newfound appreciation for the magic of flight. So spread your wings and join us on this enchanting journey – because with birds around, happiness is always in the air.
